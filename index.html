ALL PARTNERS

There has never been a more important time to organize locally.


ActLocal is a network of local leaders and organizations throughout the country that are committed to advancing a just, equitable and free America by building local power.

In the Fall of 2017, we will be holding ActLocal summits in cities and towns across the country to provide tools for coordination, training, networking, and resources to our local leaders and heroes. Each invitation-only summit will feature dynamic keynotes, skill-based volunteer matching, and master workshops. Participants will have the opportunity to elevate their campaigns, get targeted help, and network with peers and thought leaders during the event and at local follow up dinners. There are a number of ways that you can get involved. We are currently looking to connect with:

  • leaders of progressive national and local non-profit groups;
  • organizers of progressive local chapters or groups;
  • potential speakers, training providers, volunteers, and local partners;
  • people with access to space who are willing to host.

Our goal is to build local progressive power by:

  • Breaking down silos in the fight for justice and democracy;
  • Supporting cross-movement collaboration, coordination and innovation through a shared calendar and network;
  • Create electoral power by building awareness of local election cycles and timetables.

We believe that if work together to protect and empower our own communities, build our leadership skills, and lay down the infrastructure to support each other near and afar, we will be unstoppable.
