ALL PARTNERS

There has never been a more important time to get organized, especially at the local level.


We are building a network of local leaders throughout the country that are committed to protecting the human rights and civil liberties of their fellow Americans, and progressing as a nation. We belive that if work together to protect and empower our own communities, build our leadership skills, and lay down the infrastructure to support each other near and afar, we will be unstopable.

We are starting by convening a day of progressive leadership summits in cities across the country in the Fall of 2017. There are a number of ways that you can get involved. We are currently looking to connect with:

  • potential hosts
  • leaders of local progressive organizations
  • leaders of local chapters or groups
  • potential volunteers
  • people with access to space
